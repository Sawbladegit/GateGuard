Instructions to use GateGuard

Visitor

If you are a visitor, pressing the "I'm a Visitor" button will take you to the registration area. Upon registration, you will be awarded the role of a "standard visitor" and receive a "standard gatepass" to gain access to the premises. You can become an "appointment visitor" by registering an appointment with KMIT administrative personnel, and receive an "appointment gatepass". Appointment details can be edited if necessary before booking, and the registered details will be sent via email or text message.

Administrator

If you are an administrator at KMIT, you need to log in with your credentials after pressing the "I'm an Administrator" button. After logging in, you can verify, accept, reject, and maintain your appointments. You can also change your schedules, which will be reflected in the visitor's appointment booking interface. You can manipulate your schedules, change account credentials, and will be notified of everything regarding your account on GateGuard.
